Ingredients:
 - chicken wings

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 160 degrees C for 5 min. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking
    glass.
 
 2. Then put pasted tomato pest inside and 3 spoon olive oil inside and mix it with the chicken wings until they are covered with.
 
 3. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out
    clean. Cool completely.
